FAQ

  1. Please practice before betting real money


  2. Whenever you see a card displayed to the right (2-6) press the right arrow key to add +1 to the count.


  3. Whenever you see a card displayed to the left (10-A) press the left arrow key to minus -1 to the count.


  4. To be fast enough to use this in a live session, practice negating. (e.g. Ace and 4 negates to Zero)


  5. Cards not shown are intentionally unlisted (7-9) because they have a count of 0.


  6. Ideally, you want to start the count at the beginning of the shoe otherwise the count won't be completely accurate.


  7. If the count goes to above +8 increase bets, if the count goes to 10 and above (and is closer to the middle/end of the shoe) bet large


  8. If there are less cards in the shoe, the count is more accurate and gives a bigger advantage. (E.G. a +8 count at the beginning of the shoe has a higher variance than a +8 count near the end of the shoe)


  9. Counting cards does not guarantee a win, it just reduces the house edge and potentially gives you a statistical advantage.


  10. Card counting has the highest expected value when used with basic strategy


  11. Gamble responsibly and only play with what you can afford to lose
