FAQ

  1. Whenever you see a card displayed to the right (2-6) press the right arrow key to add +1 to the count.

  2. Whenever you see a card displayed to the left (10-A) press the left arrow key to minus -1 to the count.

  3. Cards not shown are intentionally unlisted (7-9) because they have a count of 0.

  4. Ideally, you want to start the count at the beginning of the shoe otherwise the count won't be completely accurate.

  5. If the count goes to above +8 gradually increase bets, if the count goes to 10 and above (and is closer to the middle/end of the shoe) bet large

  6. Counting cards does not guarantee a win, it just reduces the house edge and potentially gives you a statistical advantage.

  7. Gamble responsibly and only play with what you can afford to lose
